Pig Dice

Rules of play:

  • Two standard dice are rolled.
  • If neither die is a one, their sum is added to the turn total.
  • If a single one is rolled, the player loses thier turn score and is forced to end thier turn.
  • If two ones are rolled, the player loses thier total score and is forced to end thier turn.
  • The first player to reach 100 (or value of your choice) is the winner.
